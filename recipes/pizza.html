Description

Pizza is delicious food loved by many for its seemingly perfect combination of tomato sauce, cheese, bread, and assorted toppings. Now, you can make your own!

Ingredients

These are the items needed to create your masterfeast!

  • Tomato paste
  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Any additional toppings you desire!

Directions (Step-by-step)

  1. Roll out your dough
  2. Slather it up with that good tomato sauce!
  3. Shred a large portion of mozzarella cheese and layer it evenly on top of your sauce layer
  4. Peel and crush 2 cloves of garlic
  5. Add your toppings on top, layering evenly once again
  6. Bake in the oven at 450 degree for approximately 20 minutes or until the cheese is golden brown
